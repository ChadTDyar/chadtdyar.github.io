About Chad

Performance begins with identity. This is the story behind the systems.

Chad T Dyar is a multi-genre author, strategist, and creative performance coach who builds systems for becoming - becoming clearer, braver, better built.

He’s written nine books across business, theater, and children’s literature, all united by a single thread: transformation through clarity. From scaling enablement programs to scripting full-length plays, his work blends structure with story to help people do the work that matters.

He lives at the beach with his partner and their three dogs - Wick, Maya Rudolph, and Benny Hartz - where they grow things, stay healthy and fit, sing and dance often, and most of all, focus on being great dog dads. 🐾

Coaching Assessment Center

Discover your coaching profile with these quick, insightful quizzes.

What Kind of Coach Am I?

Discover your primary coaching approach and style.

1. How do you approach the initial conversation with your coachee?

2. How do you identify your coachee's needs?

3. What is your main focus during coaching sessions?

4. How do you measure coaching success?

5. How do you set goals with your coachees?

What Are My Coaching Strengths?

Identify your key strengths and superpowers as a coach.

1. What is your strongest skill as a coach?

2. How do you help your coachees develop new skills?

3. What do you do best during coaching sessions?

4. What do you consider your coaching superpower?

5. What feedback do you receive most often from your coachees?

What Are My Coaching Weaknesses?

Identify areas for growth and development in your coaching practice.

1. What feedback do you least like to receive?

2. What is your biggest challenge in coaching?

3. What do you struggle with the most during coaching sessions?

4. What is the most challenging aspect of feedback for you?

5. What do you need to improve the most in your coaching?

What Is My Coaching Style?

Understand your unique approach and methodology as a coach.

1. How do you start your coaching sessions?

2. What is your primary coaching goal?

3. How do you motivate your coachees?

4. What is your coaching superpower?

5. What do you believe is the most important outcome of coaching?

Who Would I Be the Best Coach For?

Find out what type of coachees you're most effective with.

1. What type of coachee do you feel most comfortable working with?

2. How do you tailor your coaching approach?

3. What is your main goal when coaching a coachee?

4. What do you find most rewarding about coaching?

5. What type of coachee do you find most challenging?
