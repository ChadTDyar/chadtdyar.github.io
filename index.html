About Chad

Chad T Dyar is a genre-jumping author, strategist, and creative performance coach with a past life in opera and a present obsession with helping people become who they are.

He’s written eight books across business, stage, and children’s genres—each rooted in clarity, creativity, and transformation. He lives at the beach with his partner and their three black dogs—Wick, Maya, and Benny—where they grow a garden and live a life built around becoming.
