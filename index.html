About Chad

Chad T Dyar is a genre-jumping author, strategist, and creative performance coach with a past life in opera and a present obsession with helping people become who they are.

His work spans eight books—business, stage, and children's—each rooted in clarity, creativity, and transformation. He's the architect behind The Enablement Operating System, the co-author of The Way I Work with AI, and a lifelong storyteller who believes systems should serve the soul—not just the schedule.

Whether designing AI frameworks, writing plays about queer becoming, or capturing the voices of his three dogs in lyrical picture books, Chad builds experiences that hold.

He lives at the beach with his partner and their three dogs—Wick, Maya Rudolph, and Benny Hartz. Together they tend a garden of fresh vegetables, flowers, and hard-won freedom. This site is where systems meet story, and performance becomes personal.
