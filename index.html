About Chad

      

        Performance begins with identity. This is the story behind the systems.       

    
         
      
               
      
        

          Chad T Dyar is a genre-jumping author, strategist, and creative performance coach with a past life in opera and a present obsession with helping people become who they are.         

        

          He’s written nine books across business, theater, and children’s literature—each rooted in clarity, creativity, and transformation. From designing enablement systems to writing plays about becoming, his work centers voice, identity, and performance.         

        

          He lives at the beach with his partner and their three black dogs—Wick, Maya, and Benny—where they grow vegetables, watch the tides, and live a life built around becoming.

The Journey

      
        
          

Act I: The Artist

          

From stage to story—performing opera taught me how to hold a room and shape a narrative with breath, body, and intention.

        
        
          

Act II: The Strategist

          

I found business. I didn’t copy it—I rearchitected it. Sales enablement became my instrument. Process, my new score.

        
        
          

Act III: The Coach

          

Now I teach others how to perform without pretending. Real leadership doesn’t shout—it listens, designs, and holds.
