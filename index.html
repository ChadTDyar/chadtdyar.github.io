About Chad

Performance begins with identity. This is the story behind the systems.

Chad T Dyar is a multi-genre author, strategist, and creative performance coach who builds systems for becoming - becoming clearer, braver, better built.

He's written nine books across business, theater, and children's literature, all united by a single thread: transformation through clarity. From scaling enablement programs to scripting full-length plays, his work blends structure with story to help people do the work that matters.

He lives at the beach with his partner and their three dogs - Wick, Maya Rudolph, and Benny Hartz - where they grow things, stay healthy and fit, sing and dance often, and most of all, focus on being great dog dads. 🐾
